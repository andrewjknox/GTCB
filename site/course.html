ELEVATION PROFILE

  • aid station
  • cut-off control

Circular from Finestrat over the Puig Campana, Serrella and Aitana sierras. Three big blocks of climbing — the Puig Campana wall straight off the start line (in the dark), the long haul to Pla de la Casa at halfway, and the Aitana ridge from km 59 — then a jagged, descending final third that never quite stops climbing. Hover or tap the trace for spot heights and gradient; markers are the 14 controls.

Squares are the eight hard cut-offs — miss one and you are out. Trace: official GPS track; totals per the organisers: 101.7 km and 6,280 m of climbing.
